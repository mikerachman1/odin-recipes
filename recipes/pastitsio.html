Pastitsio

Description

Pastitsio is a Greek casserole consiting of layers of noodles, spiced meat, and creamy bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 lb. ground beef
  • 2 finely chopped onions
  • 4 finely chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 small can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped parsley
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 lb. pastitsio noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 Tbsp. butter
  • 5 Tbsp. flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 7 oz grated kasseri cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook pasta
  2. Once pasta is cooked drain water and add butter and cheese. Set aside until assembly.
  3. Cook onions and garlic until soft
  4. Add ground beef and cook until no longer pink
  5. Add tomato sauce, parsley, salt and pepper. Remove from heat.
  6. Melt butter in saucepan and add flour while whisking until combined
  7. Add room temperature milk slowly while whisking
  8. Continue to cook and whisk until sauce has thickened
  9. Remove from heat and whisk in cheese and eggs
  10. Assemble bottom layer of noodles, Add meat sauce, then noodles, then bechemel
  11. Bake at 350 for 35-40 mins
  12. Broil top until it has browned in spots
  13. Enjoy!
